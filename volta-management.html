Volta Management

Designing a professional digital presence for an elite football development agency — helping players progress from grassroots to professional level.

Role UX / UI Designer
Type Client Project
Year 2025
Tools Figma
Overview

Bringing an elite football development agency online.

Volta is a football development organisation that supports players from grassroots through to professional level. Their USP is a mentorship-first approach — experienced coaches who prioritise long-term player development over short-term results.

The brief was to design a clear, engaging digital experience that communicates Volta's holistic development approach, services, and vision — positioning the brand as professional, credible, and future-focused.

This was a collaborative group project. As part of a three-person team, I was responsible for the Promotional Video concept, the Player Development page, and the About Us UX/UI design.

The Challenge

Communicating a complex offer without oversimplifying it.

Volta's offering is nuanced — they are not a typical football club, but an elite agency where the methodology and mentorship model are central to their identity. A key challenge was presenting this clearly and compellingly without exposing the proprietary details that form Volta's intellectual property.

The design also needed to span multiple distinct page types — homepage, story, player development, services, and contact — each with its own goal, but bound together by a consistent visual identity rooted in black, white, and grey with strong typographic hierarchy.

Key Design Decisions

Three ideas that defined the experience.

Storytelling-Led Homepage

Rather than leading with services, we proposed a short video on the homepage that follows a player's journey from grassroots to signing a professional contract. This storytelling approach captures both authenticity and aspiration — directly reflecting Volta's developmental orientation.

Global Talent Network Map

An interactive world map visualising Volta's reach and the success of players placed worldwide. This gave the brand a sense of scale and credibility at a glance — communicating impact without relying solely on text.

Player Pathway Structure

The Player Development and Services pages were structured to clearly guide users through Volta's pathway without over-exposing methodology. Hover interactions and clear CTAs helped surface key information progressively, keeping the experience clean and professional.

Wireframes

From low-fidelity sketches to structured mid-fidelity layouts.

Early wireframes explored the structure and content hierarchy across key pages before committing to visual design. Medium-fidelity layouts then developed these ideas further — clarifying user flows, interaction patterns, and layout logic.

User Testing

Testing confirmed the core experience, with one clear area to improve.

User testing revealed that people quickly understood the purpose of the website and found the CTAs highly visible and effective. Navigation to the Player Development page was identified as the one area where clarity could be improved — users understood it existed, but it took slightly longer to locate than expected.

In response, we improved navigation hierarchy and CTA placement, and maintained strong hover interactions to support discoverability throughout the site.

Outcome

Strong client feedback and a well-received final presentation.

The client, Beng'yela at Volta, praised the team's understanding of the market and the brand's developmental positioning. Specific highlights included the storytelling homepage concept, the Global Talent Network map, and the way the player pathway and services were presented.

The project demonstrated maturity in balancing creative ambition with realistic constraints — managing scope, making design trade-offs, and approaching a complex client brief in a structured, professional way.
