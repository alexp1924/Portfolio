AI Chef App

A concept app exploring how AI can simplify cooking and personalise meal planning — helping users decide what to cook based on available ingredients, dietary needs, and personal preferences.

Role UX / UI Designer
Type Concept App
Year 2026
Tools Figma, Miro
Overview

AI that makes cooking simpler, not more complicated.

The AI Chef App is a concept project that explores how artificial intelligence can make cooking easier and more personalised. The idea behind the app is to help users decide what to cook based on the ingredients they already have, their dietary needs, and their preferences.

I focused on creating a clean, easy-to-use interface — paying close attention to user flows, layout, and accessibility to make sure the experience feels simple and helpful rather than overwhelming.

This project allowed me to combine problem-solving, user research, and visual design to create a practical app idea that uses AI in a meaningful and user-friendly way.

Defining the Experience

Five core areas to support users throughout their food journey.

To understand where AI could provide the most value, I mapped the product into five core areas. The goal was to support users throughout their food journey rather than simply provide recipes — identifying where AI could meaningfully assist with decision-making, from recognising ingredients to surfacing personalised nutritional insights.

Onboarding Preferences Meal Recommendations Recipe Discovery Ingredient Scanning Nutrition Insights
Key Design Decisions

Three principles that shaped the experience.

Ingredient-First Discovery

Rather than requiring users to search for recipes, I explored an ingredient scanning feature that allows users to photograph available ingredients and receive relevant meal suggestions. This approach reduces friction and helps minimise food waste.

Actionable AI Insights

Instead of overwhelming users with nutritional data, the AI surfaces a single meaningful insight or recommendation — making the experience feel helpful rather than intrusive.

Personalised Recommendations

Recipe suggestions are influenced by user preferences, previous meals, and nutritional patterns to create a more tailored experience than traditional recipe browsing.
