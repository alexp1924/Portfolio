Manchester United Graphics

Combining a passion for football with graphic design — creating visual content inspired by Manchester United, from player kit graphics to match lineup cards.

Type Graphic Design
Focus Sports Visuals
Year 2026
Tools Photoshop, Illustrator
Overview

Football and design, brought together.

Alongside my UX/UI work, I create graphic designs inspired by Manchester United. As a long-time fan, I enjoy combining my interest in football with my passion for design — especially when it comes to visualising player stats and match data.

This ongoing personal project gives me a creative outlet to explore visual storytelling, typography, and layout outside of product design — experimenting with styles that celebrate the club and its players.

Player Graphics

Post-match stat graphics for key players.

A series of graphics designed to highlight individual player performances — displaying key stats from the game in a bold, visually engaging format. Each graphic pairs the player's image with standout numbers from their match, making the data feel immediate and shareable.

Match Lineups

Visual lineup cards for matchdays.

Matchday lineup graphics presenting the starting eleven in a clear, structured layout — designed to be visually engaging while keeping the key information easy to read at a glance.
