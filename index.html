About me

Hello! Welcome to my Portfolio.

I'm a UX/UI designer who enjoys creating clear, easy-to-use digital experiences. I like solving problems through design and turning complex ideas into simple, practical interfaces.

During my placement year at Hawk-Eye Innovations, I gained real-world experience working with developers and product teams in a fast-paced environment. This helped me build confidence in sharing ideas, improving designs through feedback, and designing for real users.

I'm especially interested in data, sports design, and creating work that is both useful and visually strong. I'm always looking to learn, improve, and take on new challenges.

What I do

UI Design High-fidelity interfaces built with strong attention to visual hierarchy, spacing, and layout — including component libraries and design systems for consistency at scale.
UX Research User interviews, usability testing, persona creation, journey mapping, and user flows — grounding every design decision in real user needs.
Prototyping Interactive Figma prototypes that bring ideas to life early, refined through testing before a single line of code is written.
Data-Driven Design Translating complex data into clear, efficient interfaces — developed through hands-on experience designing sports analysis software at Hawk-Eye Innovations.
